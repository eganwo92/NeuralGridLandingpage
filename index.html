FAQ

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about NeuralGrid

Is this platform free to use?
Signups are free with 100k early access tokens. Tokens will be used to pay for AI agent usage.

What AI Agent can I find on the platform?
Any AI agents can be found, browse by categories to find the one you need.

Can I upload my GPT-based agent?
Yes, you can upload any LLMs AI agent on the platform, as long as it is a folder containing the required parts as per documentation provided.

What languages are supported?
The website will be based in English, but the AI agents can be of any language.

FAQ

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about NeuralGrid

How do developers get paid?
Developers will be paid by the token usage count from the users, do follow the documentation to insert a token tracker count.

Will my AI Agent code be exposed or accessed?
No, we will not be exposing your AI Agent codes or stealing it. We are only running a platform to host your code. However, there will be security checks and guardrails in placed.

Can I host my own AI Model (Foundation or Pre-trained) on NeuralGrid?
There will be additional cost if you are hosting a Big Model, You can contact our Technical/Sales Support team to find out more details.

I have hosted my own AI Agent on Cloud, can I just provide an API to your frontend for my AI Agent?
Yes, You may just provide an API instead of using NeuralGrid to host your AI Agent on Cloud.
